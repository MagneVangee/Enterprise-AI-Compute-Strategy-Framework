# AI Consulting Practice Management
**Strategic Framework for Scaling Teams from 3 to 60+ Professionals**

## 1. Phase 1: The Core (3-10 Members)
- **Focus:** High-touch strategic advisory.
- **Key Roles:** Lead Architect, Strategic Consultant, Lead Data Scientist.

## 2. Phase 2: Industrialization (10-30 Members)
- **Focus:** Standardization of AI delivery pipelines.
- **Action:** Establish MLOps standards and automated audit tools.

## 3. Phase 3: Global Scale (30-60+ Members)
- **Focus:** Domain-specific verticalization (Fintech, Telco, Public Sector).
- **Structure:** Implementation of Pod-based management with centralized R&D.
