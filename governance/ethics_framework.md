# Enterprise AI Governance & Ethics Framework
**Establishing Trust and Compliance in AI Operations**

## Core Pillars
1. **Algorithmic Transparency:** Documentation of model lineage and data sources.
2. **Bias Mitigation:** Automated Red-Teaming for LLM outputs.
3. **Regulatory Compliance:** Alignment with EU AI Act and regional federal guidelines.
