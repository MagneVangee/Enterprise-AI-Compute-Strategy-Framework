## 🌟 Executive Overview
The **Enterprise AI Compute Strategy Framework** is a premier repository designed for AI Executive Advisors and Solution Architects. It provides a standardized methodology for industrializing AI initiatives, shifting from experimental prototypes to scalable, full-stack compute ecosystems. Drawing on experience from **Cisco**, **Stanford**, and **NVIDIA**, this framework integrates economic modeling with high-performance infrastructure design.

## 🏗️ Core Strategic Pillars

### 1. Strategic Advisory Engine (Economic Intelligence)
Advanced Python-based tools for evaluating the feasibility and impact of AI adoptions.
- **TCO Modeler:** Detailed Total Cost of Ownership analysis for GPU clusters (NVIDIA H100/B200).
- **ROI Projection:** Business value modeling based on automation rates and operational efficiency.

### 2. Full-Stack AI Compute Blueprints
Reference architectures for modern AI infrastructure.
- **NVIDIA Integration:** Deployment patterns for NVIDIA NIM and Triton Inference Server.
- **Networking Optimization:** Strategic guides for high-speed AI fabric (Cisco Nexus/InfiniBand).

### 3. Governance & Enterprise Readiness
Frameworks for managing risk, ethics, and scaling consulting practices.
